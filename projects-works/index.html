RMUA (RoboMaster University AI Challenge)

​ Unlike RMUC, RMUA is a fully autonomous robot competition in which teams work together to complete tasks such as control, navigation, identification, and decision-making. After six months of hard work, we participated in RMUA for the first time and make good performance, and now we are working hard to improve the overall performance of the robots.

My works:

  1. Modify and debug the chassis and gimbal firmware, add specific chassis movements to elude enemy hits.
  2. Provide interface to the upper computer, make sure the decision module can invoke the underlying action accurately.
  3. Now developing better weapon control system, it can assess enemy movement and automatically select shooting patterns based on this information.

Disinfection robot development during COVID-2019

​ I have helped to develop a disinfection robot with Fudan University’s Fuchuang Robotics Team to help fight with the epidemic. Our robot can be deployed in customer’s scenes, through a browser-based deployment tool. It can also display itself imformation and be controlled by a built-in screen.

My works:

  1. Changes the old motor driver programs and odometer programs, add drivers for ultrasonic sensors and laser sensors with UART and I2C interface.
  2. Write the screen control program based on serial protocol, which can display the status of this robot and control the working areas, UV lamps and other components.

A large quadrotor with the light-weight 6-axis manipulator for IARC mission 9th.

The IARC Mission 9

The whole development is based on STM32 and FreeRTOS, and for the convenience of testing, it has the ground station with QT. It can do many thing with ROS moveit relying on development of the upper and lower computer communication.

And then we deployed this manipulator on a large, specially designed multi-rotors UAV, which can extend the manipulator to get the target easier.

My works:

  1. I am mainly responsible for the embedded control program in this project, designed the whole system and implement in FreeRTOS.
  2. In order to facilitate debugging, I developed the mating ground station in Qt, which can display the state of the manipulator and control it to do some simple action.
  3. Turn the firmware to C++ code from C code for future development, components can be replaced quickly because of its object-oriented nature.

A logistics UAV for China Robot Competition

Rules

​ We designed this logistics drone for the logistics UAV event of the China Robot Competition, This done is light-design and it can carry three stanard small delivery boxes lighter than 500g, and place them in specific locations relied on visual information. The drone have a tracking camera which can help to locate itself indoor, and have a depth camera which can provide obstacles imformation to the navigate program. It can also do other works by disassemble the release gear.

My works:

  1. supervise the overall design of the material part, like power system selection, mechanical design, etc.
  2. Debugging UAV electronic system and its PID parameters.
  3. Design a general task solution for releasing the delivery boxes.

“Magpie”

​ “Magpie” is a quadrotor secondary development platform, a product of LiuyaoFeimeng Company, which is based on Pixhawk 4 and designed in a tiny size. This platfrom can simply replace the upper computer, sensors and other components, and have an excellent flight indicators: self weight with battery: 1.25kg, maximum load 1.2kg, maximum hover time: 17min.

My works:

  1. Mainly responsible for the equipment selection and mechanical design supervision of this type of UAV, make it lighter and have better flight performance.
  2. Now design a simple substitute power module to lower the cost and improve intergration.

“Tinylink”

​ “Tinylink”is a communication module between PC and MCU, it consists of PC part and MCU part, The PC part is writen by C++, Thanks to its object-oriented nature, TinyLink can quickly extend protocol content. And with the support of ROS, it can publish the information from MCU rapidly.

My works:

  1. Write the PC part, and design the architecture.
  2. Formulate the basic format of the protocol.
