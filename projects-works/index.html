Projects & Works

RMUA Challenge

​ Unlike RMUC, RMUA is a fully autonomous robot competition in which teams work together to complete tasks such as control, navigation, identification, and decision-making. After six months of hard work, we participated in RMUA for the first time and showed good results, and now we are working hard to improve the overall performance of the vehicle.

My works: Modify and debug the chassis and gimbal firmware, provide interface to the upper computer. Now developing better weapon control system.

Disinfection robot development during COVID-2019

​ I have helped to develop a disinfection robot with Fudan University’s Fuchuang Robotics Team to help fight with the epidemic. Our robot can be deployed in customer’s scenes, through a browser-based deployment tool. It can also display itself imformation and be controlled by a built-in screen.

My works:

  1. Changes the old motor driver programs and odometer programs, add drivers for ultrasonic sensors and laser sensors.
  2. Write the screen control program based on UART, which can display the status of this robot and control the working areas, UV lamps and other components.

A large quadrotor with the light-weight 6-axis manipulator for IARC mission 9th.

The IARC Mission 9: http://www.aerialroboticscompetition.org/assets/downloads/mission9rules_2.03.pdf

The whole development is based on STM32 and FreeRTOS, and for the convenience of testing, it has the ground station with QT. It can do many thing with ROS moveit relying on development of the upper and lower computer communication.

And then we deployed this manipulator on a large, specially designed multi-rotors UAV, which can extend the manipulator to get the target easier.

My works: I am mainly responsible for the embedded control program in this project, and I turn the firmware to C++ code from C code for future development.

A logistics UAV for China Robot Competition

Rules: http://crc.drct-caa.org.cn/static/kindeditor/attached/file/20200920/20200920133419_13668.zip

​ We designed this logistics drone for the logistics UAV event of the China Robot Competition, This done is light-design and it can carry three stanard small delivery boxes lighter than 500g, and place them in specific locations relied on visual information. The drone have a tracking camera which can help to locate itself indoor, and have a depth camera which can provide obstacles imformation to the navigate program. It can also do other works by disassemble the release gear.

My works: supervise the overall design and systems engineering.

A quadrotor secondary development platform “Magpie”

​ This UAV secondary development platform is a product of LiuyaoFeimeng Company, which is based on Pixhawk 4 and designed in a tiny size. This platfrom can simply replace the upper computer, sensors and other components, and have an excellent flight indicators: self weight with battery: 1.25kg, maximum load 1.2kg, maximum hover time: 17min.

My works: I am the chief designer of the UAV project in this start-up company, mainly responsible for the equipment selection and design supervision of this type of UAV.

A communication module between PC and MCU –”Tinylink”

​ This project consists of PC part and MCU part, The PC part is writen by C++, Thanks to its object-oriented nature, TinyLink can quickly extend protocol content. And with the support of ROS, it can publish the information from MCU rapidly.

My works: Write the PC part, and formulate the basic format of the protocol.
